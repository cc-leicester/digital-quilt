Abstract, colourful, generative visual design by Kaushik Ganeshan.

Abstract, colourful, generative visual design is an artwork realised by the student Kaushik Ganeshan in 2021, as part of the Year 1 Digital Arts Lab assessment.

Description
This piece uses an image of a very colourful train picture I took myslef, and it turns it into an abstract piece, inserting curves and points to distort the image

When I Die by Tahirah N. Moosa

Wen I Die is an artwork realised by the student Tahirah N. Moosa in 2021, as part of the Year 1 Digital Arts Lab assessment.

Description: This is an animation with sound illustrating the poem by Jalaluddin Rumi, "When I Die".

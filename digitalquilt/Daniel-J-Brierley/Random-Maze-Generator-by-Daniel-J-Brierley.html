Random Maze Generator by Daniel J. Brierley

Random Maze Generator is an artwork realised by the student Daniel J. Brierley in 2021, as part of the Year 1 Digital Arts Lab assessment.

Description
This patch generates "perfect" mazes that are truly random through an algorithm.

Interaction
Moving mouse left and right changes the size of the maze.
Moving mouse left and right also changes the colour
Clicking re-generates the Maze
